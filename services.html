Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How does the virtual e-book preview work?

Our virtual preview feature allows you to read up to 20% of any e-book before purchasing. Simply click the "Preview" button on any book page, and you'll be able to browse through selected chapters to get a feel for the content and writing style.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept UPI payments through QR code scanning or direct UPI ID entry. We support all major UPI apps including Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, and BHIM. We're working on adding more payment options in the near future.

How can I access my purchased e-books?

After purchasing, your e-books will be available in your account library. You can access them by logging into your account and navigating to "My Library." From there, you can read them online or download them for offline reading, depending on your membership plan.

How do I publish my own e-book on your platform?

To publish your e-book, create an author account and navigate to the "Author Dashboard." From there, you can upload your manuscript, add details like title, description, and category, set your pricing, and submit for review. Our team will review your submission and get back to you within 48 hours.

What formats are supported for reading e-books?

Our platform supports multiple e-book formats including EPUB, PDF, and MOBI. You can read these formats directly in our web reader or download them to read on your preferred e-reader device or app.

How does the membership plan work?

Our membership plans provide access to a library of e-books at a fixed annual fee. Depending on your plan (Basic, Premium, or Ultimate), you'll have access to different numbers of e-books and features. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time.
